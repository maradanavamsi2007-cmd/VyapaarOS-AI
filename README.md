# 🚀 VyapaarOS AI
### India's Autonomous AI Business Operating System for Small & Medium Businesses (SMEs)

<p align="center">
  <strong>🏆 TakeOver'26 Hackathon Project</strong><br>
  An AI-powered Business Operating System that helps Indian SMEs digitize, automate, and grow using Voice AI, Intelligent Agents, OCR, Business Analytics, and Predictive Intelligence.
</p>

---

# 📖 Overview

Millions of Indian SMEs still manage their businesses using notebooks, WhatsApp chats, Excel sheets, and manual billing.

This results in:

- ❌ Missed customer follow-ups
- ❌ Poor inventory management
- ❌ Manual invoicing
- ❌ Khata (credit) mistakes
- ❌ No business insights
- ❌ No automation
- ❌ Poor financial visibility

### 💡 Our Solution

**VyapaarOS AI** is an **Autonomous AI Business Operating System** that acts like an AI employee instead of a traditional ERP.

It continuously:

- Understands business operations
- Monitors inventory
- Predicts business risks
- Automates repetitive work
- Generates reports
- Recommends next best actions
- Assists the owner using Voice AI

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Small businesses struggle because their operations are scattered across:

- Paper notebooks
- WhatsApp messages
- Phone calls
- Memory
- Excel sheets

Existing ERP software is expensive, difficult to learn, and not designed for local business owners.
